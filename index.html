Can you imagine what it would be like if aliens co-exist with the people on Earth?

It is the year 10101 and the world has changed in more ways than one.. The alien lifeforms came to earth to stay, changing the rules established on earth… become a fusion of human-alien-animal, coexisting peacefully (for now).

This planet is not even called Earth anymore like in your days. It is named Metamoodon — Meta-mood-on. Why? Coz of our core element is called Metamoodium…all life depends on it.. It nourishes us, protects us and unites us. It reminds us that underneath all the different shapes, sizes and colors, we are ONE.. You wanna know more? You’re welcome to join the Meta Moodies club. But first you need to be Metamoodified!

Simply pick a Moodie from the Meta Moodies Club collection.. You have 10101 unique forms to choose from and begin your voyage into Metamoodon.
